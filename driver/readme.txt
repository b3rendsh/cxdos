DOS drivers
===========

Supported DOS versions:
CXDOS1, CXDOS2, MSXDOS2S, IDEDOS1

Supported interfaces:
BEER IDE, SODA IDE, JIO SERVER

The driver files are manually synced between the CXDOS and
MSXDOS2S repositories and there may be small differences.

The JIO driver source is still under construction and will
be uploaded here, after official release of the JIO server.
